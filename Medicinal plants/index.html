Featured Plants

Aloe Vera

Aloe Vera: A succulent plant known for its healing properties.

Botanical name : Aloe barbadensis miller

Family:Asphodelaceae (Liliaceae)

Benefits:Reducing dental plaque, accelerating wound healing, preventing wrinkles, and managing blood sugar.

Known for its soothing properties.

Tulsi

Basil: Used in Ayurvedic medicine for its anti-inflammatory properties.

Botanical name:Ocimum basilicum

Family: Lamiaceae

Benefits:Basil helps in preventing cancer.

Basil helps in diabetes management.

Also known as Holy Basil, used in many traditional medicines.

Neem

Neem: Used in Ayurvedic medicine for its anti-inflammatory properties.

Botanical name:Azadirachta indica

Family: Mahogany

Benefits:Neem boosts immunity, promotes skin health, and has anti-inflammatory and antibacterial properties

Has antibacterial and antifungal properties.

Turmeric

Turmeric: Known for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.

Botanical name:Curcuma longa

Family:Ginger family

Benefits:Improves Digestion. Helps in Muscle Recovery.
